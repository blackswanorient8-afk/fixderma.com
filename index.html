What is it?

Nigrifix cream is a customized formulation to treat hyperpigmentation associated with acanthosis nigricans. It helps to clear the darkness and hardness on certain body parts like the neck, nape, thighs, elbows, knees, knuckles, and back. It is first of a first-of-a-kind formulation specifically designed to repair damaged skin and prevent further damage.

Backed by Science

Acanthosis nigricans causes thicker and darker patches or streaks around joints and body areas with many creases and folds. Retinol is considered the first-line treatment of acanthosis nigricans. Several researches suggest that applying lactic acid with retinol has a synergistic effect in treating darkened and patched skin.

To make the formulation best suitable for sensitive skin areas like armpits, groin, and thighs, a concentrate of premium moisturizing agents like jojoba oil and sweet almond oil is added. Using tea tree oil for the skin helps with a variety of conditions. This essential oil is rich in terpenoids to soothe the dry and irritated skin. Hence, adding benefits to the darkened and dehydrated skin.

Directions for Use

  • Apply on the affected area
  • Gently massage till absorbed
  • For best results, follow it up with broad-spectrum sunscreen

Ingredients

  • Retinol

    It is considered one of the first-line treatment options for acanthosis nigricans. This vitamin A-based ingredient is the most used and studied compound for anti-ageing.

  • Lactic Acid

    It exfoliates by loosening the bonds between dead and hard skin cells to reveal a more radiant complexion. Additionally, it works well to hydrate dry skin.

  • Urea

    It helps to loosen scaly, dead skin allowing for exfoliation and improving cellular turnover. By retaining moisture and enhancing the skin’s renewal process, urea ultimately strengthens the skin’s barrier function.

  • Tea Tree Oil

    This natural oil is a potent soothing and anti-inflammatory agent that calms irritated and itchy skin. This makes it a must for the treatment regimen of acanthosis nigricans.
The Glow Test: What does the Nigrifix Cream do for your skin?

The Nigrifix cream is a dermatologist tested body pigmentation cream which makes your skin get rid of all dark patches and dark pigmentation. It removes pigmentation which can be seen on the bends like- Neck, Elbows, and Knees.

Dark elbows and knees are super common but it’s completely normal. These areas often appear darker than the rest of your body because the skin here is thicker, and experiences more friction like leaning, kneeling, or rubbing against clothes. Also, these bends have fewer oil glands, making them more prone to dryness and pigmentation. The solution is Fixderma Nigrifix Cream. This cream shows the best results by removing dark pigmentation on areas like underarms, elbows, neck and knees. It gently exfoliates the skin by reducing dark spots & patches and gives the skin a smooth, hydrated and even skin tone.

No more using lemon & baking soda but only Nigrifix Cream

When it comes to dark and pigmented knees & underarms, we try looking out for home remedies like using lemon and baking soda. We instantly expect results and even after using it, no visible results can be seen. In this situation one should not worry about pigmentation in areas like elbows, knees and under arms. Fixderma Nigrifix Cream is the all time solution for your pigmentation problems. Just apply the cream to the affected area and massage gently till it is absorbed. For best results use daily and see visible results in just 2 weeks.

A gentle solution for Acanthosis Nigricans — because your skin deserves better.

Acanthosis nigricans is a skin condition that creates dark, thick velvety skin in body folds and creases. It usually occurs in places that no one wants to see dullness or discoloration: the armpits, knees and neck. In order to get a brighter and even toned skin color, the Nigrifix cream is a huge help. This cream contains retinol, which speeds up skin cell turnover and removes the thickened, darkened skin patches formed because of Acanthosis Nigricans. Over time, it evens out skin texture and lightens pigmentation for a clearer, even tone. Therefore, this cream can do wonders to your dark and patchy skin and turn it into bright, glowing skin. It reduces pigmentation to a major extent, so that you will have the best bright skin, and feel confident about it.

Ingredients that give you that perfect glow!

  • Retinol- It is considered one of the first-line treatment options for acanthosis nigricans. This vitamin A-based ingredient is the most used and studied compound for anti-ageing.

  • Lactic Acid- It exfoliates by loosening the bonds between dead and hard skin cells to reveal a more radiant complexion. Additionally, it works well to hydrate dry skin.

  • Urea- It helps to loosen scaly, dead skin allowing for exfoliation and improving cellular turnover. By retaining moisture and enhancing the skin’s renewal process, urea ultimately strengthens the skin’s barrier function.

  • Tea Tree Oil- This natural oil is a potent soothing and anti-inflammatory agent that calms irritated and itchy skin. This makes it a must for the treatment regimen of acanthosis nigricans.

Frequently asked questions

  1. Does Nigrifix cream really help to give even toned skin?

    Yes, Nigrifix cream is specially designed and tested by dermatologists to give your knees, elbows, and neck a brightened and even toned glow. It really works to make you get rid of all dark spots and patches from the specific areas.

  2. Can I use the Nigrifix cream on my face?

    This cream is not recommended to be used on the face because it is made for treating Acanthosis Nigricans. Using it on your face can lead to irritation and redness. So always do a patch test before use.

  3. How to use the Nigrifix cream?

    To use the Nigrifix cream, just apply the cream to the affected area and massage gently till it is absorbed. For best results use daily and see visible results in just 2 weeks.

  4. Can those who do not have dark elbows, and knees use it?

    The cream is very beneficial for those who have dark pigmented underarms, knees, neck and elbows. But even if you don’t, you can try using it on the affected areas, but always make sure to do a patch test before complete application.

  5. Can I use other skincare products with Nigrifix cream?

    Yes, you can use other skincare products with Nigrifix cream. However, avoid using other exfoliating agents to prevent over-exfoliation and irritation.

  6. Does Nigrifix cream show instant results?

    The Nigrifix cream should be used daily and the visible results can be seen in 2-3 weeks.

  7. What makes this cream so special?

    The cream is packed with ingredients like Urea, that gently exfoliates skin, Tea Tree Oil that soothes dark patches, Lactic Acid that gently lightens skin discolouration and the main ingredient Retinol that fades dark spots and smoothens skin tone.

  8. Is this cream dermatologist tested?

    Yes, the Nigrifix Cream is dermatologically tested, patent formulated & is certified and tested for safety uses.

  9. Has anyone seen results with this cream?

    Of course, 93% noticed fading of dark patches from the neck, knees, underarms and elbows with regular usage.
